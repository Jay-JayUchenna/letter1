To The Permanent Secretary Of The Federal Rep. Of Nigeria

Dear Uncle

Compliments of the season to you sir..How are you and your lovely family sir?. Hope you all are in the best of health..I convey this letter to you but thought it wise to first of all wish you and your lovely family a prosperious New year..Am very thankful to you sir for the love and cultural values that you have given to us all and am appreciative of it and will remember it a life time.. May this new year bring you and your family atlarge all the health and wealth in it..May God fulfil your wishes AMEN.. Happy New year sir...

Uncle it can't elude my memory to ask about how is your lovely Family and work fairing all this while,even without emphasising much on it am sure all is wonderfully well and great...thank God...Again I must say a big thank you for standing to make Mma's burial a success and may God stand for you all time in all areas of your life..Appreciated sir..

Sir I shouldn't put this in writing but I thought it wise knowing you are a busy man but all the same do bear with me sir..My aim of conveying this words to you is to plead your assistance as I have tried and fail, also wasted money all in the name of securing a good visa abroad, so sir am pleading for you to use your good and favorable office to find a way to secure me any visa available to UK or Canada.. pls sir I didn't meant to stress you but to be safe this time.I promise to Carter for the expenses involve if necessary sir, Just read meaning to my plead and find a means to aid me out and I promise not to disappoint or bring worries to you sir... I believe your good heart and kindness will be extended to me too sir...I pray my words finds favor in your heart sir...Thanks for your understanding as I anticipate your favorable response sir...God bless you and your family sir. My regards to all...

Yours Nephew
Uchenna...
